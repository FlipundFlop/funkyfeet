Meet your teacher

Hi! I'm a 40‑year‑old Berliner and a teacher at a local Berlin school by day — and a lifelong dancer at heart. I started Funky Feet to share that pure, giggly joy that only dancing with kids can give you.

In my classes, every child is seen, heard, and celebrated. Expect warm‑ups that feel like games, choreography built together, and a whole lot of silly faces in the mirror.

“If your kid likes to jump on the sofa to music — they're already one of us.” 💛
